About Arch Vending

Serving the St. Louis metro area for over 15 years with reliable, personalized vending solutions that larger companies often overlook.

Our Story

Founded in 2008, Arch Vending was born from a simple observation: many smaller businesses and niche locations were being overlooked by large vending companies.

We started with a mission to provide quality vending services to locations that deserved attention but weren't getting it. From small office buildings to specialized facilities, we believed every business deserved reliable access to snacks, beverages, and essentials.

Today, we serve over 500 locations across the greater St. Louis metropolitan area, maintaining our commitment to personalized service and community focus. We've grown from a small local operation to a trusted partner for businesses throughout the region.
